About Us
An Interdisciplinary Engineering Team

The NJIT Solar Car Team is an entirely student-run organization dedicated to the research, design, and construction of NJIT's full-size solar-powered electric vehicle.

Formed in spring 2017, we bring together students from every major — engineering, business, design, and more — united by a shared mission to push the limits of sustainable technology.

We compete against top universities in solar car racing, giving every member real-world experience that no classroom can replicate.

Where You Fit In

We have six specialized subteams. No experience required — all majors welcome.

🔋

Electrical

Power electronics, battery management, solar array integration, and motor control systems.

⚙️

Mechanical

Chassis design, aerodynamics, suspension, braking, and structural engineering.

💻

Software

Telemetry, embedded controls, and real-time data systems for race day.

🌞

Solar Array

Photovoltaic cell layout, MPPT design, and maximizing energy harvest.

🤝

Business & Ops

Sponsorships, logistics, outreach, budgeting, and competition strategy.

🎨

Design & Media

Visual identity, vehicle wraps, social content, and team documentation.

Our Mission

"Design, create, and compete — empowering the next generation of engineers through solar innovation."

We believe in learning by doing. Every student gains hands-on experience that no classroom can replicate.
